## This Week's Term: Claude

**The quick version:** Anthropic's AI assistant. Like ChatGPT's more careful, thoughtful cousin who actually reads the whole document.

**The deeper version:**

If ChatGPT is the fast-talking salesman who always has an answer, Claude is the thoughtful friend who actually pauses to think before responding. Made by a company called Anthropic (founded by former OpenAI researchers), Claude is an AI assistant that's known for being careful, nuanced, and really good at long, complex tasks.

Where Claude really shines is handling big documents. You can drop in an entire book, a 50-page legal contract, or a massive codebase, and Claude will actually work through it carefully. It's also known for being more honest about what it doesn't know â€” less likely to confidently make things up.

## Why This Matters

Because having options matters. ChatGPT is great, but it's not the only game in town. Claude is often better for long-form writing, careful analysis, and coding tasks.
